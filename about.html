About the Project

What this platform is, how it works, and why we built it.

← Back to Home

The AI Resume Analyzer is an intelligent system built to solve one of the biggest challenges in hiring: sorting through hundreds of resumes manually. Normally, looking at every single application takes hours. This platform automates that entire process, reading through multiple PDF resumes at once and instantly matching them against a specific Job Description to find the best candidates.

Who Benefits From This?

For HR & Recruiters
  • Saves Hours of Time: Instead of opening and reading resumes one by one, recruiters can upload dozens of files at once and see instant results.
  • Smart Ranking: The system automatically extracts key skills, education, and years of experience to rank the best candidates at the very top.
  • Eliminates Bias: Candidates are judged strictly on their data, skills, and matching background, creating a fair screening workflow.
  • AI Insights: Generates instant, focused breakdown reports highlighting why a candidate fits or misses the core job criteria.
For Job Seekers & Users
  • ATS Optimization Checks: Helps users test their resumes against real-world job criteria to see if an automated system can read their details properly.
  • Instant Keyword Gap Analysis: Highlights which essential industry skills or tech keywords are missing compared directly to the target job description.
  • Clear Success Scoring: Gives an immediate transparency breakdown with an explicit percentage score, helping users fine-tune their profiles.

How It Works Behind the Scenes

1. Text Reading: The system securely processes the uploaded PDF resumes and extracts the raw text layer without messing up the formatting boundaries.
2. Cleaning & Extracting: It wipes away unneeded formatting clutter (like messy text URLs, links, and weird symbols) and captures core details like email addresses and contact pathways.
3. Scoring Engine Matrix: It runs the cleaned words against a smart dictionary lookup to calculate scores out of 100%. It grants up to 40% for Technical Skills, 20% for Education, 20% for Experience Level, and 20% for Keyword Overlap with your Job Description.
4. Instant Shortlisting: If a candidate's total ATS Score hits 60% or more and their direct text match crosses 30%, the system automatically tags them as Shortlisted. Otherwise, they are kept on a secondary review list.
